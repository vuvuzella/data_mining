Teofisto Guingona III on the ability of hackers to send $81 million from the Bangladesh central bank to a Philippine bank, Rizal Commercial Banking Corporation (RCBC).

From the RCBC, the money in the name of a businessman, William So Go, was later placed in the account of four fictitious persons and eventually found their way to casinos and unidentified hands. The possibility of tracing the $81 million or P3.7 billion is ‘’doubtful,’’ Guingona said.
